What Crown Wins Are
A Crown Win is a special type of victory in Fortnite. If you win a match, you drop into your next game with a Victory Crown. If you win again while you have your crown, you earn a Crown Victory.

Crown Wins Counter:
Your total number of crown victories appears as an emote you can use to flex.
The more crown wins you have, the more you can show off your skill.
